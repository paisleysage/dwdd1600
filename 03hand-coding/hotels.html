Hotels & Transportation

There are 3 types of on-property hotels and resorts at Walt Disney World. These categories are Value, Moderate, and Deluxe. All on property resorts have a quick-service resturant, a gift shop, free and paid activities, pools, early entry to the parks, and free transportation. There are a few types of transportation, depending on the resort. When choosing your resort it is important to take into account the location of the resort, the transportation options, and your budget.

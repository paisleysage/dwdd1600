Terms to Know

My Disney Experience App

This is the app that you will need to navigate your entire trip. This is where you can view wait times for rides, show times, characters, and make mobile orders.

Moblie Ordering

At most Quick Service Restaurants there will be an option for mobile ordering. You will choose a pickup time, and place your order. Then when it is your pickup time and you are at the restaurant, you push "prepare my order", and it will send your order to the kitchen. When the order is ready, it will tell you where to pick it up. This is often much faster than waiting in line, and you can place your order hours in advance, or place it as soon as you're ready to eat. When placing a mobile order, there are often fewer options to modify certain food items.

Quick Service Dining

These are the "fast food" restaurants of Disney. They often have mobile ordering, and low wait times. Perfect for a quick, easy, cheap meal.

Table Service Dining

Table Service Restaurants are going to be a little more expensive, and take more time. You need a reservation as well. Reservations can be made 60 days in advance, but for some you could get a reservation day of. Most also have walk-up availability, depending on how busy the restaurant is. The food will be served either family style, buffet style, or a la carte.

Character Dining

Charcater Dining is just a Table Service Restaurant, just with character meet and greets. They will be more expensive, but they are so magical. They usually take a lot longer, as you have to wait for each individual charcater to come to your table.

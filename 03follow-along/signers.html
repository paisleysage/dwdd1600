Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famously gave the largest signature.
Thomas Jefferson
Primary author of the Declaration.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned polymath and diplomat.
John Adams
Later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Samuel Adams
Prominent revolutionary leader.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution for independence.
Roger Sherman
Only person to sign all four major founding documents.
Charles Carroll
The only Catholic signer.
Robert Morris
Financier of the Revolution.
Francis Lewis
Known for his service and sacrifice.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration, an act considered treasonous by the Brittish Crown.

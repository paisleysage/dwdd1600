Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is often underrated, and labeled as a half-day park. This is because it doesnâ€™t have very many rides, but it still has so much to offer! It is an accredited zoo, and is a huge player in the conservation and preservation of our planet. The theming is beautiful and the most cohesive out of the four parks. There are incredible shows, fun experiences, lots of valuable learning opportunities, and it is breathtaking at night. It is a celebration of the beauty and wonder of nature, and is dedicated to educating people so that they learn to love and care about our world. Over the next few years there will be a lot of construction, but there is still a lot to do.
